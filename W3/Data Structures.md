### postfix notation and infix notation arithmetic expressions
- infix notation arithmetic expression: 4 + 3 ∗9
- postfix notation arithmetic expression: 4 3 9 * +

Example:
***x y w z / − ∗*** is means ***x ∗ (y/w − z)***.
在运算除法时，比如 a b /，应当是 a / b。
